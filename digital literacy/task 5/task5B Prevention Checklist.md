**Part B – Prevention Checklist**



**Stay Safe Online Tips**



* Always verify the sender before clicking any link
* Do not share OTP, passwords, or bank details
* Use strong and unique passwords
* Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
* Check website URLs carefully before entering details
* Regularly update apps and devices
* Be cautious of urgent or suspicious messages



**UPI / Financial Safety (Important)**



* Never share UPI PIN with anyone
* Double-check payment requests before approving



**Report Cybercrime**



* Website: cybercrime.gov.in
* Helpline: 1930
