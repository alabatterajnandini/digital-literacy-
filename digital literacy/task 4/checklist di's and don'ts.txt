Checklist for Responsible Social Media Use
5 Do’s:

Do think before posting – always consider how your content reflects on you as a student and future professional.
Do respect others’ privacy and obtain consent before posting photos or information about friends or classmates.
Do maintain a positive and constructive tone in all your online interactions.
Do verify facts and sources before sharing news or information to avoid spreading misinformation.
Do use privacy settings wisely and regularly review who can see your posts and profile.

5 Don’ts:

Don’t post anything you wouldn’t want your professors, future employers, or family to see.
Don’t engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or spreading hate speech towards any individual or group.
Don’t share confidential academic or personal information (yours or others’) publicly.
Don’t use social media during class hours or while completing assignments.
Don’t accept friend/follow requests from unknown people or share your location in real-time.
